Hi internet friend, I'm Jennica ◡̈

After the pandemic scrambled my plans of pursuing a career in fashion merchandising, I, funnily enough, found UX design through a Codecademy sorting quiz while trying to figure out what I might enjoy—something between art, since I was always creative as a kid, and technology, as the go-to tech wizard in my family. I got into Columbia, enrolled in interaction design, gambled on maybe liking it, and ended up absolutely loving it. What started as a detour turned out to be the best redirection towards a career that I genuinely enjoy and four years later, I’m still wholeheartedly enjoying every pixel of it.

Outside of design I'm currently learning how to sew, brain dump journaling in the mornings, trying to drink more water, and baking desserts! I do also occasionally enjoy:

  • Eating out at pretty brunch spots
  • Going to coffee shops just to order matcha or chai
  • Bahn mi sandwiches with extra extra veggies
  • Farmers and flea markets
  • Endlessly scrolling through Ebay for vintage goodies

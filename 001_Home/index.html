About

So nice to e-meet you, call me Jennica (hover here)! I’m the product designer you’ve been scouring the internet for.

Here’s a quick rundown: I found out about ux through a Codecademy quiz, gambled on maybe liking it in college, fell in love with with the fact that we can use design to solve actual problems; a couple internships, contract jobs, and trash-fulls of sticky notes later, we’re here! I’m eager about the future of product, forever seeking growth as a designer (and as a human), and currently exploring different ways of making tangible things.

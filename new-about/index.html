Hi internet friend, I’m Jennica! I’m a “will try anything” type of designer based in Seattle, more formally trained in digital product design.

After discovering what user experience design even was through a Codecademy quiz (lol), I was immediately intrigued by its blend of design, technology, and psychology. I went on to earn my Bachelor of Arts in Interaction Design, User Experience at Columbia College Chicago, taking a chance on a new field I quickly grew to love. Now as more of a multidisciplinary designer, still primarily working in product, I try my best to keep learning about other design specializations. Currently, I’m down the rabbit hole of email marketing and development.

Outside of design, you can find me knitting, brain dump journaling in the mornings, trying to drink more water, and remixing dessert recipes!

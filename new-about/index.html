Jennica, Product Designer

Work // About

Jennica is a “pretty much everything” designer based in Seattle, more formally trained in product design.

After discovering user experience design through a Codecademy quiz, Jennica was immediately drawn to its blend of art and technology. She pursued a degree in Interaction Design & User Experience at Columbia College Chicago, taking a chance on a field she quickly fell in love with. Now, transitioning more into a multidisciplinary role, she's actively expanding her design skills — currently dabbling in visual design and email marketing.

Outside of design, Jennica is knitting, brain dump journaling in the mornings, trying to drink more water, and remixing dessert recipes!

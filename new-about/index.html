Jennica, Product Designer

Work // About

Jennica is a “will try anything once” type of designer based in Seattle, more formally trained in digital product design.

After discovering what user experience design even is through a Codecademy sorting quiz, Jennica was instantly drawn to its blend of art, technology, and psychology. Taking a chance on a field she quickly fell in love with, she earned her B.A. in Interaction Design & User Experience at Columbia College Chicago. Now, transitioning more into a multidisciplinary role, she's actively expanding her design skills — currently dabbling in visual design and email marketing.

Outside of design, Jennica is knitting, brain dump journaling in the mornings, trying to drink more water, and remixing dessert recipes!

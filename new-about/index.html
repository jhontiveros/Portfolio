About

Jennica is a multidisciplinary designer based in Seattle, more formally trained in product design.

After discovering user experience design through a Codecademy quiz, Jennica was immediately drawn to its blend of art and technology. She studied Interaction Design, User Experience at Columbia College Chicago, taking a chance on a field she quickly grew to love. Now a multidisciplinary designer, she moves between specializations, always eager to learn. Currently, she is dabbling in visual design and email marketing/development.

Outside of design, Jennica is knitting, brain dump journaling in the mornings, trying to drink more water, and remixing dessert recipes!

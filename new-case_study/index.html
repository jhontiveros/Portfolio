Optimizing gasoline data dashboard tooling @ Costco

The data science & analytics team at Costco had an existing report tracking gasoline sales volumes at all locations worldwide across different grades of fuel. However, the report lacked helpful visualizations, making it hard to understand and communicate user needs effectively.

Abstract: At Costco, I led the redesign of the gasoline sales/forecasting dashboard to improve data visualization and decision-making tools. I collaborated with the data science team to identify user pain points through UX audits, interviews, and affinity diagrams, then iteratively designed wireframes within Power BI’s constraints based on stakeholder feedback. The final design introduced key features like location-specific analysis and sparked discussions on refreshing design guidelines to enhance future data reports.
